Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal Treatment

    A procedure to remove infected pulp from inside the tooth, relieving pain and saving the natural tooth.

  • Teeth Whitening

    A cosmetic treatment that brightens and removes stains from your teeth for a whiter smile.

  • Teeth Extraction

    The removal of severely damaged or decayed teeth to maintain overall oral health

  • Teeth Replacement

    Restores missing teeth using dentures, bridges, or implants for improved function and appearance.

  • Gum Treatment

    Helps manage gum diseases like gingivitis and periodontitis to ensure healthy gums.

  • Dental braces

    Orthodontic devices used to align and straighten teeth for a healthier bite and better smile.

  • Oral Hygiene

    Preventive care that includes regular cleaning, brushing, and dental checkups to maintain a healthy mouth.

  • Live Advisory

    Real-time consultation with dental professionals to answer questions and guide treatment.

  • Paediatric Dentistry

    Specialized dental care focused on the unique needs of children and young patients.

  • Teeth Cleaning & Polishing

    A dental procedure that removes plaque, tartar, and stains from teeth, leaving them clean, smooth, and shiny.

About Us

We Care For Your Dental Health

Located at Kiambaa, Kiambu County Trace Dental Clinic, is your ultimate walk-in facility for all dental services. We aim to provide affordable and professional dental care to all clients.

Our dental procedures are tailored to meet all our customers’ needs while utilizing the latest cutting edge technology and infection control protocols. With over 10 years’ experience in dental care, we aim not just to alleviate the pain but to improve overall quality of life for all. We invite you to join our mission of sharpening smiles and touching lives as you experience holistic and comprehensive services at amazingly affordable prices.
